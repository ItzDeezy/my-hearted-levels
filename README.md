# My Hearted Levels

Never lose track of your favorite creations again. **My Hearted Levels** adds a heart button to every level in your My Levels list, letting you mark the ones you care about most and filter down to just them instantly.

## Features

- **Heart any level** — a heart button appears on every cell in your My Levels list
- **Filter view** — toggle the heart filter button to show only your hearted levels
- **Paginated favorites** — if you have more than 10 hearted levels, they paginate just like normal
- **Persistent hearts** — your hearted levels are saved between sessions
- **Session filter** — the filter state carries over when you view a level and come back, and resets cleanly when you relaunch the game

## How to Use

1. Open the **Create Tab**
2. Tap the **heart button** on any level cell to mark it as a favorite
3. Tap the **heart filter button** on the left sidebar to show only your hearted levels
4. Use the **arrow buttons** to page through your favorites if you have more than 10
